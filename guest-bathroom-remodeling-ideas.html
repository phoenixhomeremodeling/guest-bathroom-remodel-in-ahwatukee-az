How much is a 10x10 bathroom remodel?

On average, a complete small bathroom remodel can range from approximately $70 per square foot for low-end fixtures with DIY labor to around $250 per square foot for higher-end fixtures completed by a licensed contractor, resulting in a total cost of up to $10,000 or more for the renovation.

How often should showers be replaced?

When deciding between refinishing and replacement for your shower, consider that a typical shower has a lifespan of 10 to 15 years, making refinishing or reglazing a suitable option before Year 15, after which breakages tend to become more frequent.

What is the average cost of a bathroom remodel in Arizona?

The average cost of a bathroom remodel in Phoenix, Arizona, typically falls within the range of $6,000 to $15,000, and if you're looking for ways to avoid spending around $10,000 on a full bathroom renovation, there are strategies you can employ to save money.

Are bathroom remodels worth it?
