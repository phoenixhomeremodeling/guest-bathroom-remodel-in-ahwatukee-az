Frequently Asked Questions

Can you remodel a bathroom in 3 days?

The timeline for a bathroom remodel may extend up to four days when sharing a remodeler with other clients, whereas if you are the sole client, you can anticipate a shorter duration of one to two days.

Is DIY bathroom remodel worth it?

In summary, a bathroom remodel will typically cost less than half of what a general contractor (GC) would charge, as it allows you to keep the material costs while saving on labor expenses, but the decision to hire a GC may be justified for the convenience it offers, which can be considered a luxury worth the additional cost.

How long does a DIY bathroom remodel take?

A bathroom remodel typically requires between two and three months to complete, although unexpected discoveries during the removal of old tile may impact the timeline, yet with proper planning, DIY bathroom repairs can be undertaken with confidence.

What is the best color for a guest bathroom?

To establish a tranquil, spa-like atmosphere in your bathroom, consider using calming neutral shades such as blue, green, and gray, drawing inspiration from nature with hues like soft sage, sky blue, and ash gray, or, for a cozier ambiance, opt for warmer tones like pale yellow, warm beige, or a subtle blush color to impart a welcoming radiance to the space.
