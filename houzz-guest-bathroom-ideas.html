Frequently Asked Questions

How much value does a bathroom remodel add to a house?

Indeed, a bathroom renovation has the potential to increase home value, with a midrange remodel yielding a 70.1% return on investment and an upscale remodel offering a 60.2% return, as indicated by Remodeling magazine's 2020 Cost vs. Value report.

What is a high end bathroom?

High-end bathrooms are typically tailored entirely to the homeowner's preferences and are characterized by luxurious finishes and design elements, such as stone countertops, double vanities, stylish mirrors, dual sinks, makeup counters, and other premium features.

Is DIY bathroom remodel worth it?

In summary, a bathroom remodel will typically cost less than half of what a general contractor (GC) would charge, as it allows you to keep the material costs while saving on labor expenses, but the decision to hire a GC may be justified for the convenience it offers, which can be considered a luxury worth the additional cost.

Can you do a bathroom remodel yourself?

With thorough planning, a well-defined budget, and appropriate assistance, a DIY bathroom remodel is a feasible project, but it's essential to conduct comprehensive research, adhere to your budget while maintaining flexibility, and make early decisions regarding your color scheme, fixtures, and finishes.
